Tennis is the first of the five games featured on Wii Sports and Wii Sports Club. The game's objective is to hit the ball over the net and make it bounce twice on the opposing team's side.

The main gameplay involves using the Wii Remote like a racket and swinging it as the ball comes toward the player.
