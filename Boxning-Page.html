Boxing is one of the five games featured in the Wii Sports series.

The objective of Boxing is to reduce your opponent's health meter and get a K.O. to win the match.

To get a K.O. the player must weaken the opponent and then knock him/her on the ground.

The opponent must stay on the ground for 10 seconds to win the round.

The player has 3 rounds to accomplish this. If either player/CPU can't K.O the other during the three rounds, a decision will be made post-match to determine who the winner will be based on their performance.
