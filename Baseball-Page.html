Baseball is one of the five available games to play in Wii Sports and Wii Sports Club.

One game consists of three innings, each team taking a turn at batting and a turn on the field when three outs are acquired.

The main gameplay of the sport is either holding the Wii Remote like a bat and swinging at thrown baseballs or throwing the baseball using different types of throws.
